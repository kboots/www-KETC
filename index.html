Welcome To Our Tennis Club !

Established in 1968 Keilor East Tennis Club. In the hub of the Keilor East sports precinct with soccer pitch, scout hall, Keilor East Leisure Centre, Essendon Keilor Collage. The only tennis club in the area with 6 cushioned hard courts

    Competitions

    The Keilor East Tennis Club offers competition tennis in following associations and is affiliated with Tennis Australia.

  • North suburban tennis association (Saturday afternoons)

  • North suburban night tennis association (Tuesday ladies, Wednesday mixed, Thursday mens) North Suburban Night Tennis Association

  • North suburban junior association (Friday night, Saturday mornings) North Suburban Junior Tennis Association

  • North suburban ladies association (Thursday daytime) North Suburban Ladies Tennis Association

  • Tennis Victoria Pennant - mens and ladies (Saturday or Sunday)

    • Coaching

    The Keilor East Tennis Club offers intensive after school coaching programs for all ages and standards

  • Hotshots tennis - Junior beginner group coaching
  • Advanced group coaching
  • Junior advanced squad training
  • Intermediate / senior /advanced squad
  • Individual coaching (private lessons)
  • Semi - individual coaching (partner coaching)
  • Adult coaching
  • Hitting partners
  • Cardio tennis - fitness based

    • There is a coaching group for everyone
